The Best Movies According to Asher

My top 3 movies of all-time

1st Place - Terminator 2

With the AI being on the conversation everywhere, watching this epic movie 1991 will probably evoke totally different feelings to people who watched it before. And for people who haven't watched that movie yet - why do you hate life? You have Arnold Schwarzenegger as a machine that comes from the future to save our world. What else can you possibly look for in a movie?! Go on, watch it, I'll wait.

2nd Place - Pursuit of Happiness

Isn't happiness what we all look for? Well, we say that but most of us just go and live our lives the way other people would expect us too - if it's where we live, what we decide to study, what career path we choose. Well, in that movie Chris Gardner is someone who's looked for opportunities to find happiness and eventually finds it thanks to the hardships life has thrown at him. Well, maybe it would be more accurate to say he took life's gift, and it was a gift thanks to his character.

Fight Club

Well trying to recommend this movie without ruining the experience is a real challenge. So, all I can say is you have an insomniac office worker that lives a very boring life meets someone who is exactly the opposite of him. From the day they meet the office worker life changes to say the least. Oh, and did I mention Brad Pitt is in that movie? You probably stopped reading this recommendation once you read "Brad Pitt" so I guess that will be a good place for me to and rewatch all those great movies myself.
